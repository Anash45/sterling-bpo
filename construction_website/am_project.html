Arzul Musheer (Pvt) Ltd is proud to announce its inaugural project, 10 Park Drive, located in the prestigious Emaar DHA Islamabad, Pakistan. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we bring you a luxurious residential development that epitomizes modern living.

Luxury Living Redefined

Experience unparalleled luxury and sophistication in our meticulously designed residential development, 10 Park Drive. Each aspect of the project has been carefully curated to offer residents an exceptional living experience.

  • Plot Size: 22,000 SQ.FT
  • Project Type: 2B+G+5
  • Ground Floor: Residential Concierge and Retail Outlets
